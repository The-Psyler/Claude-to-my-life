# Engineering Analysis & Roadmap Summary (2026-04-11)

## Overview
This document summarizes the recent deep-dive analysis of the CTML codebase, identifying critical architectural risks and proposing a phased roadmap for improvement. The goal is to transition from a "working prototype" to a "robust continuity engine."

## Identified Priority Problems

### 1. Data Integrity & Persistence (Critical)
- **Issue:** Potential for silent failures in `saveState()` due to missing `await` keywords. High risk of race conditions during rapid user interactions (e.g., quick captures).
- **Risk:** Data loss or corrupted state due to overlapping asynchronous writes.

### 2. Rendering Performance (High)
- **Issue:** Monolithic rendering functions (`renderAll`, `renderVault`) re-create large portions of the DOM on every change.
- **Risk:** UI lag, layout shifts, and degraded performance as the "Vault" grows in size.

### 3. User Experience & Interaction (High)
- **Issue:** Heavy reliance on modals for basic tasks creates high cognitive friction during daily rituals.
- **Risk:** Reduced "flow" and momentum, contradicting the app's core philosophy of "minimal friction."

### 4. Maintainability & Scalability (Medium)
- **Issue:** `src/app.js` is becoming a monolith, making it difficult to manage as new features are added.
- **Risk:** Increased technical debt and higher probability of introducing regressions during feature implementation.

## Proposed Roadmap

### Phase A: Stability & Integrity
- **Goal:** Implement a sequential write queue or semaphore for `saveState()` to prevent race conditions and ensure all writes complete.
- **Impact:** Guaranteed data persistence.

### Phase B: Architectural Refactoring (ES Modules)
- **Goal:** Break `src/app.js` into native ES modules (`state.js`, `ui.js`, `storage.js`) without requiring a build step.
- **Impact:** Improved maintainability and cleaner separation of concerns.

### Phase C: Performance Optimization
- **Goal:** Implement granular, component-based rendering and "lazy loading" for large lists (Vault/Playbook).
- **Impact:** Smooth, high-performance UI regardless of data volume.

### Phase D: UX Polish
- **Goal:** Introduce CSS transitions for screen switching and expand inline editing capabilities in the "Work" view.
- **Impact:** Enhanced ritualistic "flow" and reduced friction.
